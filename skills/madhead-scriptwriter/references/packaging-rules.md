# Packaging Rules

Generate a full packaging bundle after the script.

## 1. Title Ideas

Provide 5 title ideas.

### Formula
[SHOCK CAPS - 1 to 3 words]! [Specific military subject] [strong verb] [weapon name or technical detail]

### Rules
- Maximum 12 words when reasonably possible
- Include a weapon, military unit, or technical identifier
- Create urgency and curiosity

## 2. YouTube Description

Format:
- one short hook line under 150 characters
- one natural paragraph of about 100-120 words
- exactly 5 important keywords in bold
- timestamps block
- subscribe line
- safety disclaimer

## 3. SEO Hashtags

Provide 10 hashtags.
Do not make them generic.
Balance weapon names, operation names, geography, and broader military themes.

## 4. Extended Tags

Provide 20 total tags.
This set should include the main hashtag concepts plus related weapon names, places, people, events, and military-technical terms.
