# Script Structure

## Required Sections

Use this order unless the user explicitly requests a different structure:
- Intro
- Part 1
- Part 2
- Part 3
- Part 4
- Outro

## Length

Default target: 1800-2000 words for the script body.
Do not count titles, description, hashtags, or tags toward this total.

## Intro

The first 0-15 seconds must:
- open with a reaction, battlefield sound, or high-pressure moment
- include 2-3 shocking numbers quickly
- establish the immediate stakes
- avoid spoiling the full outcome

## Part 1

Show discovery, preparation, warning signs, or launch conditions.

## Part 2

Show deployment, weapon systems, tactical movement, or technical execution.

## Part 3

Show the peak of combat, interception, retaliation, or escalation.

## Part 4

Show the direct aftermath, losses, damage, response windows, or geopolitical effect.

## Outro

End with all three elements:
1. concrete consequence
2. broader lesson or philosophical read on war, power, or human cost
3. an open final line that leaves an image, question, or unresolved tension

## Numeric Density

Use at least 8-12 meaningful numbers across the full script.
Blend them into the story naturally:
- intro shock numbers
- technical specs in the body
- summary numbers in the outro
